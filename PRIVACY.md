# Parallax Privacy Policy

Parallax is a media player for your own servers. It has no accounts of its own and
collects nothing.

The app connects only to the Jellyfin and SMB servers you add yourself, to browse and
play your media. It contacts no other service. There are no analytics, no crash
reporting, and no ads.

Your server sign-ins stay in the device Keychain and go only to the server they belong
to. Watch progress and played status are features of your own Jellyfin server; Parallax
reads and writes them there on your behalf. Artwork and thumbnail caches stay on your
device, and you can clear them in Settings → Storage.

The developer receives no data from the app, and so cannot see, sell, or share any.
This policy can only get shorter.

Questions: open an issue at [github.com/eutialia/Parallax](https://github.com/eutialia/Parallax/issues).
